Daily Bazer BD!


Daily Bazer BD is a start-up Many producer and distribution company. Its strategy is to serve the upscale niche markets of the ecommers industry.There are many untapped potential markets within this industry that desire high-quality, stylish goods, but do not think they can afford them or do not know where to purchase them.

It is the firm's intention to build and export its products to the Bangladesh and to make Electric parts the number one selling in Dhaka. To do this the company's long-term goals are to to achieve a 20% market share in the Bangladesh, build brand image and brand equity through marketing, achieve a sustainable 55% profit margin.
